Brownie Recipe

Brownie

Delicately crisp on top with a rich, fudgy center these brownies offer a deep, smooth chocolate flavor in every satisfying bite —simple to love, hard to forget.

Ingredients :-

  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips – 1 cup (about 170g)
  • Unsalted butter – ½ cup (1 stick or 113g), melted
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (100% cocoa) – ½ cup (about 43g)
  • Eggs – 3 large eggs
  • Salt – ½ teaspoon
  • Granulated sugar – 1 cup (200g)
  • All-purpose flour – ¾ cup (95g)
  • Pure vanilla extract – 1½ teaspoons

Steps :-

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line an 8×8" metal baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Melt butter, then whisk in sugar and cocoa powder until smooth.
  3. Add eggs, vanilla extract, and salt; whisk well.
  4. Fold in flour until just combined, then stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 35–40 minutes.
  6. Let cool completely before slicing and serving.
